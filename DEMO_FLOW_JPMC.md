# JP Morgan Chase Demo Flow

## Overview

End-to-end walkthrough demonstrating the Banker Viewer platform — from document ingestion through AI-powered client engagement.

---

## Step 1: Landing — User Enters the Web App

- The banker (Dave's president, **Ben**) opens the web app.
- Clean landing screen is displayed, ready for action.

---

## Step 2: Document Upload

- Ben **drags and drops** a batch of documents into the web app.
- Multiple files land in the upload zone at once.

---

## Step 3: Document Analysis (Prototype Animation)

- The app kicks off an **analysis animation** — a polished prototype transition showing documents being processed.
- Visual feedback as each document is parsed, categorized, and indexed.
- Once complete, the UI transitions smoothly to the next step.

---

## Step 4: Connect the CRM

- Ben is prompted to **connect the CRM** integration.
- CRM connection flow completes, syncing client data into the platform.

---

## Step 5: Add a New VIP — Alexandra

- Ben **adds a new VIP client**: **Alexandra**.
- Using **Tab autocomplete**:
  - `Tab` → fills in **interest/preferences**
  - `Tab` → fills in **email address**
  - `Tab` → fills in remaining profile fields
- Each tab press intelligently auto-completes the next field based on CRM data and AI inference.

---

## Step 6: Alexandra's Profile — Document Recommendations

- Ben navigates into **Alexandra's profile**.
- The platform displays **AI-generated document recommendations** — documents relevant to Alexandra based on her profile, interests, and history.

---

## Step 7: Add Comments & Send Documents

- Ben selects the documents he wants to send to Alexandra.
- He **clicks on the comments section** and adds personalized notes/comments to the document.
- He **sends the document** out to Alexandra.

---

## Step 8: Alexandra's View — Receiving the Document

- **Perspective switch**: we now open **Alexandra's view**.
- Alexandra sees the document that was sent to her.
- She can see the **comments that Ben left** for her — inline annotations and notes on the document.
- She reads through the comments.

---

## Step 9: Mobile Walkthrough

- Alexandra opens the document on **mobile**.
- She enters a **guided walkthrough** of the document on her phone.
- She navigates through the key sections step by step.

---

## Step 10: Alexandra Replies (Tab Autocomplete)

- Alexandra wants to **reply** to the document/comments.
- A reply tab/section is available.
- Using **Tab autocomplete**:
  - She starts typing a response and presses `Tab` to auto-complete her reply.
  - The AI suggests contextually relevant responses based on the document content and Ben's comments.
- She sends her reply.

---

## Step 11: Alexandra Chats with the AI

- Alexandra opens the **AI chat interface**.
- She asks questions about the document — clarifications, deeper dives, related topics.
- Using **Tab autocomplete** throughout the conversation:
  - `Tab` auto-completes her questions.
  - `Tab` suggests follow-up queries.
- The AI provides intelligent, document-aware answers.

---

## Flow Summary

| Step | Actor     | Action                                | Key Feature              |
|------|-----------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| 1    | Ben       | Opens web app                         | Landing screen           |
| 2    | Ben       | Drops documents                       | Drag & drop upload       |
| 3    | System    | Analyzes documents                    | Prototype animation      |
| 4    | Ben       | Connects CRM                          | CRM integration          |
| 5    | Ben       | Adds VIP Alexandra                    | Tab autocomplete         |
| 6    | Ben       | Views Alexandra's profile             | AI recommendations       |
| 7    | Ben       | Comments & sends documents            | Inline comments          |
| 8    | Alexandra | Receives & reads document + comments  | Client document view     |
| 9    | Alexandra | Mobile walkthrough                    | Mobile experience        |
| 10   | Alexandra | Replies using Tab autocomplete        | AI-assisted replies      |
| 11   | Alexandra | Chats with AI about the document      | AI chat + Tab complete   |
